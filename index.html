Security basics

Short checklist every user should follow.

  • Only use the official app and verified links.
  • Keep your recovery phrase offline; never type it on a website.
  • Verify addresses on the device screen before sending.
  • Enable biometric/PIN and keep your device firmware updated.

FAQ

How do I update firmware?

Open the companion app, navigate to Settings → Device → Firmware, and follow prompts with your device connected.

Which networks are supported?

Most major networks (BTC, ETH, and select EVM chains) are supported via the companion app. Check the latest compatibility list in-app.

Can I restore on a new device?

Yes. Choose Restore during setup and enter your recovery phrase on the device only.
